Rob's Strawberry Margarita Recipe

The Best Frozen Marg You've Ever Made

Tired of making margs that are too sweet? Not sure how to get the right proportions so it has just the right kick? Need something to mask a disaster meal? This frozen stawberry marg will answer all your prayers.

Ingredients

Note: I use my can of limeade as my measuring device.

  • 2 heaping handfuls frozen strawberries
  • 1 can Minute Maid frozen limeade
  • 1 can of Camarena tequila
  • 1/3 can orange juice
  • 1/3 can GranGala triple sec orange liquor
  • 1/3 Rose's sweetened lime juice
  • 3-4 cups ice
  • lime wedges for garnish
  • margarita salt
  • chilled glasses

Steps

  1. Blend all the liquids and strawberries for 30 seconds
  2. Add ice in batches, blending and tasting for flavor and consistency as you go
  3. Once the right consistency is reached, salt the glasses, pour the marg, and add lime wedges
  4. Serve immediately! Enjoy!
