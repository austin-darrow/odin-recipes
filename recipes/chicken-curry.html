Chicken Curry

Delicious Homemade Curry

Curry comes in many forms. But there is one requirement universal to all good curry: don't cut corners. This recipe doesn't cut the hard corners. It takes time. But the end product will be worth your effort.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thigh (cut into bite sized pieces)
  • 2 small yellow onions (diced)
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 8 oz umami mixed stir fry mushrooms (chopped)
  • 1 poblano pepper (minced)
  • 1 bunch cilantro (finely chopped)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (choose a flavorful can)
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 heaping spoonful bacon drippings
  • 1.5 tbsp red curry powder
  • 1.5 tsp ground ginger root
  • 1.5 tsp cumin
  • 1.5 tsp ground coriander
  • 1.5 tsp turmeric
  • 1.5 tsp cayenne pepper
  • salt (to taste)
  • Garam masala (to taste)
  • Juice of 1 large lemon
  • 1.5 cups of Jasmine rice

Steps

  1. Add the avocado oil to a nonstick pan over high heat. Salt the chicken, then add in batches. Cook until golden, crispy bits form on the chicken and start sticking to the pan.
  2. Add olive oil, butter, and bacon drippings to the pan. Cook the vegetables until the onion starts to crisp up like the chicken.
  3. Add all the seasonings to the vegetables + 1 tbsp water. Stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken + juices, tomatoes, yogurt, 1 tbsp cilantro, and 1/2 cup of water. Bring to a boil. Coat with another tbsp of cilantro and sprinkle Garam masala over it. Cover and simmer with 20 minutes.
  5. While that simmers, cook white rice as a side.
  6. When chicken is cooked through, serve over rice with lemon juice added on top.
